Additional transparency criteria are provided for specific human rights issues. Ensuring respect for human rights in supply chains is relevant to some extent to all large companies. Further specific issues are provided for the Energy and ICT sectors. Whether these issues are salient further depends on an individual company's operational context. Therefore the assessment also provides information on how many companies identify these issues as salient and commit to prevent or mitigate adverse impacts.
